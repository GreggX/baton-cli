5Edge cases — failure & boundary states · 4 treatments

Three of the seven edges were already designed in upstream: multi-zone jump ⇒ one coalesced notice for the final zone (2a) · downgrades shown like upgrades + dismissals re-armed (2a · 2d) · explicit no-candidates report naming the rules checked (3c). New here: 5a stale vs unknown — both stamped with the age of the last good reading (ticking live), no unsubstantiated zone ever shown · 5b invalid thresholds rejected with each problem named, defaults kept · 5c interactive — ignore every recommendation: one notice per zone entry, zero repeats, the bar stays truthful · 5d one monitor ↔ one session; a second session gets its own instance.
